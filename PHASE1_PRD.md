# Village Phase 1 PRD — Seed

## Mission

Help the user see what they are doing with their life today.

## Core loop

Choose what matters → live the day → record what happened → reflect → close the app.

## Included

- Email/password and Google authentication
- Gentle onboarding: improvement areas and enjoyable activities
- Habits: daily / selected weekdays, yes-no and measurable goals
- Per-habit streak and lifetime journey metrics
- One-time tasks
- Sleep start/wake tracking and consistency summaries
- Today aggregation with yesterday comparison
- Daily / weekly / monthly journey summaries
- Lightweight Village Seed state: Quiet, Resting, Stable, Growing, Blooming

## Excluded from Phase 1

- Coins, XP, purchases, reward shop
- Complex village simulation, NPCs, pathfinding, farming
- Social feeds, public profiles, leaderboards
- Wearable-derived medical sleep claims
- AI coaching
- Complex recurrence rules and recurring tasks

## Phase 1 Definition of Done

A user can create an account, describe what matters and what they enjoy, create habits/tasks, track sleep, use the app for several days, compare personal progress across time, and see a calm village state that reflects recent rhythm without judgment.
